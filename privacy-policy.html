Your Rights

Even though this service does not collect data directly, you should be aware of your rights under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) and General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) when interacting with third-party services:

  • Access: Request access to your data from any third-party service.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data held by third-party services.
  • Erasure: Request the deletion of your data if it is no longer necessary.
  • Restriction: Request restrictions on processing under specific circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data, including for marketing purposes.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact the relevant third-party service directly.
