The map indicates the geographical locations with the best conditions for hunting porcini mushrooms (and species that grow under similar conditions).

The locations are indexed on a colour scale from pale yellow to dark red, indicating the conditions for porcini mushroom hunting from less to more favourable.

The scale is supplemented by a numerical rating from 0 to 10 as illustrated below.

Functions:

Identify Mushroom Type with Machine Learning Model
Centres the Map on the User's Location
Switch between Light- and Dark-mode
Displays Numeric Rating
Opens General Info and Instruction Page
Opens Patreon Page to Support the Project

Press and Hold on the Map for Two Seconds:

Open the destination in Maps
